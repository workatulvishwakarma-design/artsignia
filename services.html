  • Environmental control

Specialized Services

Lighting Design

Architectural and ambient lighting solutions — from facade illumination to interior mood lighting — designed for aesthetic impact and energy efficiency.

MEP & AV Integration

Comprehensive mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and audio-visual system design and coordination — ensuring seamless integration with the built environment.

Project Management

End-to-end project management including BOQ structuring, value engineering, multi-vendor coordination, quality assurance, compliance, and on-site supervision.

Fit-Out & Turnkey

Full-scope interior fit-out with furniture, finishes, flooring, false ceiling, partitions, and branded elements — delivered as a turnkey solution.

Compliance & Statutory

Regulatory compliance management including fire safety, building codes, accessibility standards, and environmental clearances — handled end-to-end.
